About Me

Hello! I'm M. Sameer Khan, a dedicated teacher, trainer, and materials scientist with a strong academic background and practical expertise in advanced materials and thin film technologies. With a Master's in Materials Engineering and a Bachelor's in Metallurgical Engineering from NED University, I specialize in developing multifunctional coatings with UV-blocking and anti-reflective properties.

Alongside my research, I have taught at both school and university levels, designing science labs, conducting practicals, and mentoring students in chemistry and materials science. My experience in synthesis, coating techniques, and material characterization (SEM, XRD, FTIR, WVTR, etc.) is complemented by a passion for education and continuous learning. I thrive on combining scientific innovation with effective teaching to inspire future engineers and scientists.
